Make it you

Make it you is a new business in Auckland city offering a unique service of hand crafted, personalised invitations suited to any occasion. Unlike printed invitations, each one is crafted by hand to your exact specifications!

You’re able to specify the colour, size, theme etc all to how you want – making an invitation specifically designed for you and something you won’t find any where else.

Having invitations like these can add that extra WOW factor to any event your planning, whether it be a Wedding, Birthday, Anniversary, Baby shower etc – the options are endless.

Whether you’re having a small celebration with family, or planning a massive event with hundreds of guests, Make it you will craft every card with attention and detail to make it just how you want it.

Each card is individually made and subject to availability
