Wedding

Click to flick through pages

This handcrafted Scrapbook is personalised to my own wedding and shows examples of a few
different styles that could be incorporated into your own scrapbook.

You could choose to do it with each page representing a different aspect of the
wedding (in this case starting with before the wedding and going through each
part of the day with the last page incorporating the original wedding invite,
save the date magnet, name tags etc used on the day itself as a keepsake).

You can choose to use many different colours and have each page completely
different like this one, or you could choose a few colours and keep the
exact layout for every page.

There are so many options so please enquire about your own one today!
